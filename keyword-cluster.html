Recommended content order

Publish and internally link the homepage first because it contains the tracking utility. Next, build status meaning and COD pages because these answer problems users have after tracking. Then expand city pages from the office cluster such as PostEx office Lahore, PostEx office Karachi, and PostEx office Islamabad. Each city page should link back to the tracking homepage and to the office hub page. Avoid thin duplicate pages; add city-specific support notes, delivery expectations, and FAQs.
