How to find the nearest PostEx office safely

Courier branches, pickup points, and operational hubs can change because companies shift routes, merge service points, or update city coverage. The safest way to confirm a PostEx office near you is to keep your consignment number ready, check the latest status on the tracking page, and contact official PostEx support or the seller who booked the parcel. If you are a merchant, the seller dashboard or account manager is usually the fastest route for pickup and return questions.

For customers, most parcel issues do not require visiting an office. Track the shipment first, confirm whether it is received, in transit, enroute for delivery, waiting for delivery, delivered, or returned, and then contact support with the exact tracking number. This page is internally linked with the PostEx tracking tool, status guide, and COD tracking guide so you can move from location intent to parcel intent quickly.
