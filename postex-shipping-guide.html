Shipping Guide

PostEx parcel shipping and delivery guide

This guide covers the simple actions that reduce delivery confusion: save the tracking number, confirm your phone number, understand the status, and contact the right party when a parcel is delayed.

For customers

Ask the seller for the PostEx tracking number, use the tracking search before calling support, keep your phone active, and prepare COD payment when the parcel is out for delivery.

For sellers

Share tracking numbers clearly, keep customer address details clean, monitor delayed shipments, and link buyers to the tracking page to reduce support load.

Internal resources

Start with the PostEx tracking homepage for live results. Use the status guide for confusing updates, the COD tracking guide for cash-on-delivery parcels, and the office near me page for city-level service help.
